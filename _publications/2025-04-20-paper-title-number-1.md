## Project Overview: Reproducing the Tree of Thoughts (ToT) Framework

While Large Language Models (LLMs) excel at sequential text generation, they often struggle with complex, multi-step reasoning that requires strategic planning. This project reproduces the Tree of Thoughts (ToT) framework (Yao et al., 2023) to address these limitations. Moving beyond the linear constraints of Chain of Thought (CoT), ToT treats reasoning as a search over a tree of intermediate "thoughts." By implementing a structured branching and evaluation process, this reproduction demonstrates how LLMs can look ahead, backtrack, and explore multiple solution paths to solve problems that require deliberate heuristic search.
