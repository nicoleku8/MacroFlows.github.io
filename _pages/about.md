## Hi, thank you for visiting my blog!

I'm interested in **Macro Trading**, specifically focusing on **systematic and discretionary strategies** within **interest rate products**. 

> My philosophy is simple: all traders are fundamentally macro traders. 

My current work involves analyzing the relationship between **stock index options**, **central bank policies** and **fixed-income products**. 

---

### I am particularly interested in:

* **Regime Detection:** The change of asset relationship in shifting market environments.
* **Derivative Dynamics:** Gaining technical proficiency in pricing and hedging option instruments.
* **Macro Depth:** Understanding the relationship between central bank policy and cross-asset volatility.
